## 🚀 How to Use the Updated Project

### **Start Here**

1. Open the root **index.html** file in your browser
   - This is the new landing page
   - Beautiful introduction with feature highlights
   - Easy navigation to all quizzes

### **Run Quizzes**

1. From the landing page, click "Start Quiz"
2. OR directly open `Quiz/quiz.html`
3. Select a subject (Computer, Math, or Physics)
4. Read the introduction and click "START QUIZ"
5. Answer questions and get instant feedback
6. View your score and restart if desired

### **Send Feedback**

1. Click the "Feedback" link in the navbar
2. Fill out the feedback form
3. Submit your feedback

### **View About**

1. Click the "About Us" link in the navbar
2. Learn about the project
3. Links to quiz and feedback form
